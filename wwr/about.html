Our mission is to provide thrilling and safe rafting adventures for all ages.
Experience the excitement, build lasting memories, and join our community of adventure seekers!

History

Founded in 1998 by outdoor enthusiasts Mark and Sarah Johnson, Rapids Water Wonders began as a small operation with just two rafts and a passion for sharing the beauty of river adventures. What started as weekend excursions for friends quickly grew into a full-fledged business as word spread about our exceptional safety standards and unforgettable experiences.

Over the past two decades, we've introduced over 50,000 adventurers to the thrill of whitewater rafting while maintaining our commitment to environmental stewardship and community engagement. Our guides are among the most experienced in the industry, with many having been with us for over 10 years.

Today, we continue to expand our offerings while staying true to our roots - providing authentic, exhilarating river experiences that connect people with nature and each other.
