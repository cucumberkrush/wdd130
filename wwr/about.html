Our mission is to provide exhilarating and safe white water rafting experiences while preserving the natural beauty of our rivers.

History

Founded in 1985 by river enthusiasts, White Water Rafting has grown from a small family operation to one of the premier rafting companies in the region. We pride ourselves on our safety record and commitment to environmental stewardship.
